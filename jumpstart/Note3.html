Recipe Section

    Ingredients

  • Large Granny Smith apples (1/2 inch slices)
  • Large Honey Crisp apples (1/2 inch slices
  • Large Golden Delicious apples (1/2 inche slices)
  • Pie crusts
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg

    Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400F
  2. Mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Put all mixed ingredients into pie crust
  4. Bake the pie for 45 minutes or until brown and crispy
